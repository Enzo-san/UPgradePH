Basic Accounting

The Basic Accounting Program will equip you with introductory accounting, fit for beginners and fundamental to the mastery of advanced accounting. The program is taught by some of the best college students in the country since all our instructors are from University of the Philippines Diliman, Ateneo de Manila University, De La Salle University Manila and University of Santo Tomas.
