Introductory Physics

The Introductory Physics Program equips the students with tutorials on elementary physics, perfect for Engineering students. The program is taught by some of the best college students in the country since all our instructors are from University of the Philippines Diliman, Ateneo de Manila University, De La Salle University Manila and University of Santo Tomas.
