Basic Accounting

The Basic Accounting Program will equip you with strong fundamentals in accounting. This is highly recommended for those taking up business courses. We have all the exercises prepared for you that you will surely see in your future accounting courses. Being fundamentally sound in accounting is very important and that is why we would like you to build them with us.

The program will start on June 21, 2021. This is designed to run for 45 hours (8 weeks), with 2-hour sessions every Monday, Wednesday and Friday (MWF), or Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday (TTHS), depending on the student’s schedule.

Available platforms for the tutorial sessions are  Zoom and   Google Meet.
