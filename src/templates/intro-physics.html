Introductory Physics

The Introductory Physics Program equips the students with the basics of physics. This is highly recommended for all students taking up Science, Technology, Engineering and Math (STEM) programs, coming from other academic strands or students who want to build strong fundamentals on physics. This program is taught with many illustrative examples and exercises for the mastery of the subject. This program can be taken together with our Differential Calculus program.

The program will start on June 21, 2021. This is designed to run for 50 hours (8 weeks), with 2-hour sessions every Monday, Wednesday and Friday (MWF), or Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday (TTHS), depending on the student’s schedule.

Available platforms for the tutorial sessions are  Zoom and   Google Meet.
