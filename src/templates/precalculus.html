Precalculus

The Precalculus Program equips students with fundamental knowledge in precalculus which is a prerequisite for future calculus courses. This is highly recommended for those enrolling in Science, Technology, Engineering and Math (STEM) programs who have yet to take up precalculus in high school. This program is taught with many illustrative examples and exercises for the mastery of the subject.

The program will start on June 21, 2021. This is designed to run for 45 hours (8 weeks), with 2-hour sessions every Monday, Wednesday and Friday (MWF), or Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday (TTHS), depending on the studentâ€™s schedule.

Available platforms for the tutorial sessions are  Zoom and   Google Meet.
